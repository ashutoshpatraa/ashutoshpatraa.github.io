About AIT Chemistry Classes

AIT Chemistry Classes is a premier institute in Vadodara, Gujarat led by Ashwin Sir, offering comprehensive chemistry coaching for Classes 9-12, AP Chemistry, JEE, and NEET. We provide both in-person and online classes, serving students globally including USA. Known for concept clarity and exceptional exam performance.

"Teaching is my passion – Ashwin Sir"

Courses Offered

  • Class 9 & 10 Chemistry Foundation
  • Class 11 & 12 Chemistry (CBSE, GSEB, IB & Cambridge)
  • AP Chemistry (Advanced Placement)
  • JEE Main & Advanced Chemistry
  • NEET Chemistry Preparation
  • Online Classes for International Students (USA)
  • Crash Courses / Doubt Sessions
